My research focuses on incremental image recognition algorithms in open environments, primarily addressing the catastrophic forgetting problem in continual learning of deep learning models.

### ğŸ¯ Incremental Image Recognition in Open Environments (2021.09 - Present)

Designing image recognition models based on **large-scale pre-trained models** to address the challenges of **dynamic and diverse data distributions and modalities** in open environments. The core focus is on developing **continual learning** mechanisms that enable models to incrementally learn new data while effectively retaining stable memory of old knowledge (i.e., solving the **catastrophic forgetting problem**), thereby significantly improving the generalization and adaptability of models in the open world.
