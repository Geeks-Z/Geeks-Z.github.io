About Me

I am Hongwei Zhao , a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Beihang University , advised by Associate Prof. Rui Liu and Prof. Yong Chen.


My research focuses on Incremental Image Recognition in Open Environments , aiming to address the catastrophic forgetting problem in deep learning models during continual learning, enabling models to incrementally learn new knowledge while effectively retaining old knowledge.

Research Interests

üéØ Incremental Image Recognition in Open Environments

2021.09 - Present

Designing image recognition models based on large-scale pre-trained models to address the challenges of dynamic and diverse data distributions and modalities in open environments. The core focus is on developing continual learning mechanisms that enable models to incrementally learn new data while effectively retaining stable memory of old knowledge.


  • Hybrid fine-tuning of PTM based on MoE and LoRA to address Prompt hyperparameters and Adapter computational complexity
  • Designing multi-teacher distillation strategies based on LoRA for few-shot incremental learning
  • Designing hierarchical Prompt generators based on LLM for multi-modal incremental learning
Continual Learning LoRA Vision-Language Models MoE

üöÅ UAV Path Planning for General Aviation

2018.09 - 2021.03

  • Accurate modeling : Using grid method to map 3D point clouds to matrices for precise obstacle modeling
  • Minimum energy objective : Establishing minimum energy consumption objective function with UAV performance and wind constraints
  • Hybrid algorithm : Designed improved ant colony and A* hybrid algorithm, reducing planning time by 96.6%
